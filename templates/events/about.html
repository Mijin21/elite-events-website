One of India's most sought-after Event Management and stage production company based in Chennai. With two decades of experience from events to entertainment to engagement and Media, across industries and businesses, We have but one goal- To push the boundaries of what's possible, to keep the experience at the forefront and spark human connection and most importantly bring in celebrated artists and never-before thought of engagement concepts.

Our in-house team gathers the requirements from the client after which our creative team of experts and designers conceptualise the theme and visual magnificence for the same. Our artistic and technical expertise join together to bring events to life and provide our clients with an unparalleled customised experience. With every event we orchestrate, we empower a whole community that includes our team members and vendors comprising of the stage fabrication team, carpenters, moulders, welders, artists, painters, drivers, house keeping staff, furniture suppliers, LED and AV equipment suppliers, genest, sound and light teams, florists, manpower liaising teams, caterers and logistic partners just to name a few. This feeling of empowering the entire eco-system with every work that gets converted is our most rewarding contribution to the industry.

Whether you're hosting a holiday dinner for ten guests or a conference for thousands, we ensure that every aspect from the most obvious to the finest details meets your level of satisfaction. We have no cuts, no retakes , no post production luxuries for our on stage events like any film production but we guarantee the same 3 hour entertainment or more to carry back experiences for a lifetime

Get ready to keep the spreadsheets and presentations aside for a night of glitz and glam at "Corporate Celebrities," an event designed to celebrate the hidden talents lurking within the workforce. Inspired by the vibrant energy of college cultural events and the electrifying rehearsal sessions, which are totally lacking in the corporate industry, this competition offers a platform for employees to shed their professional personas and embrace their artistic sides. Whether you're a vocal powerhouse, a dance floor dynamo, a shutterbug with an eye for capturing moments, a budding filmmaker with a story to tell, or have a secret flair for the runway, Corporate Celebrities has a category where you can shine.
